About this area

San Diego

Located by the ocean , Comfort Inn and Suites San Diego - Zoo SeaWorld Area is in
San Diego's Mission Valley neighborhood, an area with good airport proximity and
shopping. Ocean Beach Pier and San Diego Coronado Bridge are notable
landmarks, and the area's natural beauty can be seen at Old Town San Diego State
Park and Mission Bay. Hotel Circle and SeaWorld are not to be missed. Our guests
appreciate the hotel's location for the sightseeing.

About this property

Comfort Inn and Suites San Diego - Zoo SeaWorld Area

Suburban San Diego Hotel in Mission Valley , walk to Hotel Circle

Along with an outdoor pool, this smoke-free hotel has a fitness center and a spa
tub. Buffet breakfast is free. Other amenities include WiFi in public areas, self
parking, and coffee/tea in a common area. All 199 rooms provide conveniences like
refrigerators and coffee makers, plus WiFi and flat-screen TVs with cable channels.
Free weekday newspapers, free local calls, and hair dryers are among the other
amenities that guests will find.

Comfort Inn and Suites San Diego - Zoo SeaWorld Area offers 199 air-conditioned
accommodations with coffee/tea makers and complimentary weekday
newspapers. 32-inch flat-screen televisions come with premium cable channels.
Bathrooms include shower/tub combinations, complimentary toiletries, and hair
dryers.

In-room wireless Internet access is available for a surcharge. Business-friendly
amenities include desks along with free local calls (restrictions may apply).
Additionally, rooms include irons/ironing boards and blackout drapes/curtains.
Housekeeping is offered daily and hypo-allergenic bedding can be requested.
